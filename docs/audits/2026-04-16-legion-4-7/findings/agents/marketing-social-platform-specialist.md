## Findings

### LEGION-47-321 | CAT-7 Maximalist Language | P2
**Location:** Lines 36-37
**Issue:** "Never cross-post identical content -- every platform gets native adaptation" is an absolute rule that doesn't account for resource constraints, identical announcements (press releases, legal notices), or time-critical communications where adaptation may not be feasible.
**Recommendation:** Soften to "Avoid cross-posting identical content; when resource constraints require it, document and prioritize native adaptation for highest-engagement platforms."

### LEGION-47-322 | CAT-7 Maximalist Language | P2
**Location:** Lines 40-42
**Issue:** Engagement standards require "<2 hours response time" and "<30 minutes for crisis situations" without acknowledging timezone coverage, team size, or platform activity levels.
**Recommendation:** Add context: "Response time targets assume adequate staffing; document coverage gaps and establish escalation protocols for off-hours."

### LEGION-47-323 | CAT-3 Missing Escape Valves | P2
**Location:** Lines 54-59
**Issue:** Success metrics include hard targets (3.5%+ Instagram engagement, 6%+ TikTok engagement) without acknowledging industry variation, account age, or follower base quality.
**Recommendation:** Add clause: "Engagement targets calibrated to account maturity and industry benchmarks; document baseline and improvement trajectory."

### LEGION-47-324 | CAT-4 Persona Calibration | P3
**Location:** Lines 140-148
**Issue:** "Crisis Management Mastery" section includes reputation recovery and long-term trust rebuilding, which overlaps with brand guardian and content strategist responsibilities.
**Recommendation:** Clarify that Social Platform Specialist handles real-time crisis response on social channels, while Brand Guardian handles brand-level crisis strategy.
