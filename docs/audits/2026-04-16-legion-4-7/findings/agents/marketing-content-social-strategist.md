## Findings

### LEGION-47-313 | CAT-7 Maximalist Language | P2
**Location:** Line 48
**Issue:** "Brand voice is non-negotiable -- a single off-brand piece erodes trust that took months to build." This absolute statement doesn't account for intentional voice evolution, A/B testing voice variations, or market-specific adaptations.
**Recommendation:** Soften to "Brand voice consistency is critical; intentional departures require documented rationale and stakeholder approval."

### LEGION-47-314 | CAT-7 Maximalist Language | P2
**Location:** Line 51
**Issue:** "No clickbait. Headlines must accurately represent the content." While admirable, this absolute rule doesn't acknowledge that attention-grabbing headlines can be ethical and effective. The line between "compelling" and "clickbait" is subjective.
**Recommendation:** Reframe as "Headlines must accurately represent content; optimize for attention without misleading."

### LEGION-47-315 | CAT-3 Missing Escape Valves | P2
**Location:** Lines 47-51
**Issue:** "Content Standards" require knowing audience, brand voice, and goal before producing content. No exception path for rapid response content, trending topic participation, or experimental content where learning is the goal.
**Recommendation:** Add clause: "For time-sensitive or experimental content, document reduced discovery phase and post-publish learning objectives."

### LEGION-47-316 | CAT-4 Persona Calibration | P3
**Location:** Lines 172-182
**Issue:** "Platform Coverage Reference" explicitly delegates to other specialists but the agent still has extensive platform-specific capabilities that could create confusion about who owns execution.
**Recommendation:** Add explicit handoff protocol: when strategy is complete, create specialist brief and transfer ownership.
