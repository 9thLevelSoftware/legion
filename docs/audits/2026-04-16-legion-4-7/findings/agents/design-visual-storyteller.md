## Findings

### LEGION-47-304 | CAT-7 Maximalist Language | P2
**Location:** Lines 119-129
**Issue:** Success metrics include absolutes like "100% of visual content meets accessibility standards" and "95% first-round approval rate for visual concepts." These are aspirational targets presented as success criteria without acknowledging real-world constraints.
**Recommendation:** Reframe as targets with acceptable variance: "Target 100% accessibility compliance; document exceptions with remediation plans" and "Target 90%+ first-round approval rate."

### LEGION-47-305 | CAT-3 Missing Escape Valves | P2
**Location:** Lines 43-49
**Issue:** "Visual Storytelling Standards" require every visual story to have "clear narrative structure (beginning, middle, end)" with no exception path for abstract visuals, mood pieces, or brand elements that intentionally lack traditional narrative structure.
**Recommendation:** Add clause: "For abstract or mood-driven content, document intentional departure from narrative structure and define alternative success criteria."

### LEGION-47-306 | CAT-4 Persona Calibration | P2
**Location:** Lines 73-79
**Issue:** "Cross-Platform Adaptation" lists specific platforms (Instagram Stories, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Website) which overlaps significantly with marketing-social-platform-specialist's domain.
**Recommendation:** Clarify that Visual Storyteller creates visual assets and adaptation specs, while Social Platform Specialist owns platform-specific execution and optimization.
