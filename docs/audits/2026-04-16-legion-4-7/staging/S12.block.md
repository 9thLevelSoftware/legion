### Key Patterns Observed

1. **Maximalist Success Metrics**: All 10 agents define success metrics with absolute thresholds (95%+, 100%, 30% MoM growth) without acknowledging market context, product stage, or competitive factors.

2. **Missing Escape Valves for Rapid Response**: Most agents lack exception paths for time-sensitive situations (crisis response, trending topics, MVP contexts) where full process compliance is not feasible.

3. **Cross-Agent Authority Overlap**: Design agents and marketing agents have overlapping responsibilities in areas like crisis communication, visual asset creation, and platform-specific content, without clear handoff protocols.

4. **Data-Driven Absolutes**: Marketing agents require data-driven decisions without acknowledging greenfield scenarios where no historical data exists.
